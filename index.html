Understand PCOS

PCOS is a hormonal disorder characterised by excess androgen production, affecting the overall health of women.

Symptoms of PCOS

Irregular or missed periods

Acne, oily skin, & unwanted hair growth

Unexplained weight gain or difficulty losing weight

Hair thinning or infertility

Causes of PCOS

Genetic factors

Insulin resistance leads to hormonal imbalance

Lifestyle factors such as poor diet & stress
